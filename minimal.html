Minimal

Scroll down to learn about modern interior design styles

Minimalist interior design is very similar to modern interior design and involves using the bare essentials to create a simple and uncluttered space. It’s characterised by simplicity, clean lines, and a monochromatic palette with colour used as an accent.

It usually combines an open floor plan, lots of light, and functional furniture, and it focuses on the shape, colour and texture of just a handful of essential elements.

Characteristics of Minimal Interior Design include: bare essential items, clean lines and a monochromatic colour paletes

Illustrating aesthetic restraint—a key concept in formal simplicity and architectural minimalism.
